Zach's Simple Rice Bowl

Rice. Tofu. Magic.

I'm lazy. That's why I'm a great programmer.

Life is hard enough without creating more to stress about than already comes your way.

Thus, the rice bowl rules my lunch menu.

With a rice maker, an air fryer, some tofu, some veggies, and the sauces of your choice, you're in business. The lunch business.

And business is booming.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • tofu
  • veggies
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Rinse rice in strainer until water exiting rice runs clear
  2. Add rice to rice cooker and add water to the appropriate fill line
  3. While rice cooker runs, air fry tofu in 1"x1" cubes for 15 minutes
  4. After cooking, mix rice, tofu, and veggies in bowl and serve!
  5. Go back to work!
